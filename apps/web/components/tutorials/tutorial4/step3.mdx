In the previous step, we requested 100 tokens privately from the [Miden Faucet](https://faucet.testnet.miden.io/).

The resulting private output note containing our tokens must now be consumed to fund our wallet.

Click on the <EllipsisVertical className="size-4 inline" /> icon button in the _"Consumable Notes"_ table to consume the note with your wallet.

A _"Request Import Private Note"_ modal from your Miden Wallet will show up asking you to confirm importing the note containing your tokens.

<WalletAlert />

The private note will transition to the **Consumed** state and disappear from your wallet consumable notes.
