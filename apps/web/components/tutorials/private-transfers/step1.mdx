In this tutorial, we will connect our [Miden Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/miden-wallet/ablmompanofnodfdkgchkpmphailefpb?hl=en) to the Playground and sign private transactions (mint assets, send assets to another wallet).

Install the browser extension and setup a new **Private** wallet, at the _Set Up Account Recovery_ step, select the **Guardian** option to enable Guardian-based recovery for your wallet.

The main difference between **Public** and **Private** accounts is how their state is handled: while a public account state is entirely stored on-chain, only a commitment (hash) to a private account's state is stored on-chain.

In Miden, when you are the custodian of your account state (in the case of a private wallet), **losing this state amounts to losing your funds**, similar to losing a private key.

With a Guardian protected account, a third party stores a copy of your private state that you can recover in case you lose access to your wallet but the guardian operator can see your state.

Connect your **Private** wallet by clicking on _"Select Wallet"_, you will be requested access to your **Private** account state, accept and click on _"Connect"_.
