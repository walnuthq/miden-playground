import Link from "@/components/tutorials/tutorial-link";

Once your account is deployed, <Link href={`/accounts/${props.counter?.address}`}>click on its ID</Link> to navigate to the contract details page.

On the _"Components"_ tab, you can access the account components to perform a first transaction on this newly created contract.

You can read the initial counter value by clicking on the _"Invoke"_ button next to the **get_count** procedure.

Click on the _"Invoke"_ button of **increment_count** to trigger a custom transaction and increment the counter.
