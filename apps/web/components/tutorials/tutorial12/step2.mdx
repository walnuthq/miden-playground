From the <Link href="/accounts">accounts page</Link>, click on _"Create new account"_ and select the _"Deploy multisig"_ option.

Give your multisig a name and don't add any other signers: our multisig will only have your connected private wallet as signer.

When you're done configuring your multisig, click on the _"Deploy"_ button to create your multisig wallet.
