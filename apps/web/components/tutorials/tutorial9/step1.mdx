In this tutorial, you'll create your first smart contract and understand the structure and implementation of both the counter account contract and increment note script.

Let's get started by clicking on _"Create new script"_ then make sure to name your script **counter-account** and select an **Account Component** script, do not use any example.

It's important that you name your script **counter-account** otherwise you'll have compilation errors on step 4 of this tutorial.
