Setting up a Miden Guardian secured wallet involves using 2 accounts:

- **The Guardian** is where your wallet funds are secured, by storing a backup of your private account offchain and providing an interface to update, sync and restore your private state.
- **The Signer** is a regular private wallet controlling the Guardian: it is responsible for creating it, signing transactions updating the Guardian state and as the owner, it is capable of restoring your wallet backup if you lose access to your private state.

Let's deploy a **Guardian** using our connected private wallet as a **Signer**: from the <Link href="/accounts">accounts page</Link>, click on _"Create new account"_ and select the _"Deploy guardian"_ option.

Give your new wallet backup a name and click on the _"Deploy"_ button to create your guardian-protected wallet, you will be asked to sign the deployment configuration to add your private wallet as a registered **Signer**.
