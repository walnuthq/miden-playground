Oh no! You lost your phone and your private wallet state is gone forever… or is it?

Luckily, you have configured a Guardian wallet backup and you only need to get access to your **Signer** wallet to restore your funds.

Connect your Signer wallet back and head to the <Link href="/accounts">accounts page</Link> to restore your guardian wallet by clicking on the _"Create new account"_ button on top of the page and selecting the _"Restore guardian"_ option.

A modal will show up requesting both a name and the address of the guardian account to restore, if you haven't saved your guardian account address, here it is:
