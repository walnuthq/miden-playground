Now that your guardian is created, let's fund it by requesting tokens from the [Miden Faucet](https://faucet.testnet.miden.io/).

Click on the _"Mint"_ button to request 100 tokens from the faucet.

<MintButton />

Once the mint request is completed, let's wait a couple more seconds to make sure the output note generated by the Miden Faucet has been committed to testnet network.

Notice how the note containing your requested tokens automatically appears under the _"Consumable Notes"_ section of your guardian wallet.
